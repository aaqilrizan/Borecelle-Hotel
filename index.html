BORCELLE HOTEL

About Borecelle Hotel

Borecelle Hotel has a story that's as captivating as the landscapes that surround it. Established over 7 years ago, our hotel originally served as a residence for British colonial officers during the colonial era. Its colonial architecture and lush gardens still retain the essence of that bygone era, offering a unique glimpse into the past. Over the years, the property has undergone meticulous renovations to ensure it meets the highest standards of comfort and luxury while preserving its historical charm. Today, it stands as a symbol of timeless elegance and warm Sri Lankan hospitality.We, at Borecelle Hotel, are committed to ensuring your stay in Kandy is unforgettable. From our attentive staff to our world-class amenities, we strive to create an atmosphere of tranquility and luxury that complements the natural beauty of Sri Lanka.

Swimming Pool

Experience aquatic bliss at Borecelle Hotel's inviting swimming pool. Nestled within our tropical oasis, it's a haven of relaxation and fun. With crystal-clear waters, poolside service, and a tranquil ambiance, it's the perfect place to unwind and soak up the Sri Lankan sun.
